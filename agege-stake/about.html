ABOUT US

We bring out God's best in them

Missionaries of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints are dedicated individuals who are "Called to Serve" and spread the teachings of Jesus Christ and the principles of the Church.


Typically, Young men and Young women volunteer to serve a mission lasting 18 Months for Young Women and 24 months for Young Men, during which they engage in community service, teach gospel principles, and share their faith with others.


They often learn new languages and immerse themselves in different cultures, aiming to foster understanding and build relationships while promoting spiritual growth.

OUR OBJECTIVES

We are set to achieve the following laudable objectives:

Our Primary objectives as Missionaries of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is to "Invite other to come unto Christ and recieve the restored gospel through Faith in the lord Jesus Christ and his Atonement, Repentance, Baptism, Recieving the gift of the Holy Ghost and Enduring to the end."

We aim to provide service to communities, strengthen families, and encourage moral values. Additionally, missionaries strive to support local congregations, assist in humanitarian efforts, and promote education and self-reliance among those they serve.

Our ultimate goal is to inspire faith and positive change in the lives of individuals and communities. ________________________________________
